He was born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, and played a pivotal role in India's nuclear and missile programs, significantly contributing to the nation's space endeavors. Known as the "Missile Man of India," he inspired millions with his vision for a prosperous nation and was loved for his simplicity and dedication. Kalam authored several books, including "Wings of Fire," which reflects his life and thoughts.
